A space for stillness

These verses and melodies are here to quiet the noise. Let this be your pause — a moment to reflect, breathe, and return inward.

قُلْ يَا عِبَادِيَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَسْرَفُوا۟ عَلَىٰٓ أَنفُسِهِمْ لَا تَقْنَطُوا۟ مِن رَّحْمَةِ ٱللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَغْفِرُ ٱلذُّنُوبَ جَمِيعًا ًۚا
“Say, O My servants who have transgressed against themselves [by sinning], do not despair of the mercy of Allah. Indeed, Allah forgives all sins.” Surah Az-Zumar 39:53
ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْكِتَٰبُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ ۛ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِينَِ
“This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah.” — Surah Al-Baqarah 2:2
فَإِنِّي قَرِيبٌ ۖ أُجِيبُ دَعْوَةَ ٱلدَّاعِ إِذَا دَعَانِ
“Indeed I am near. I respond to the call of the supplicant when he calls upon Me.” - Surah Al-Baqarah 2:186
